Web design case study

Ricci's Landscape Management redesign

This redesign focuses on elevating the brand into a more professional, modern, and luxury experience. The goal was to better reflect the quality of the company’s work while improving clarity, trust, and visual polish.

See the redesign direction View outcomes
Goal

Look more premium and trustworthy.

Style

Modern, refined, and luxury-inspired.

Focus

Stronger hierarchy and cleaner presentation.

Design direction

Premium outdoor spaces deserve a premium website.

The redesign shifts the brand away from a basic service look and toward a polished experience that feels elevated from the first impression.

The challenge

What needed to change

The original website had strong content, but the visual presentation needed to better communicate professionalism, credibility, and a high-end experience.

Professional look

Stronger first impression

The design needed to feel more trustworthy and established so visitors instantly understood the quality behind the brand.

Modern UI

Cleaner layout and hierarchy

The page needed a more structured layout, more breathing room, and clearer section hierarchy to improve readability and flow.

Luxury feel

Elevated visual language

The site needed to feel more refined, with premium styling that matched the sophistication of the landscaping work.

Design solution

How the redesign was shaped

The final direction blends clean modern structure with warm, luxury-inspired details so the site feels polished without losing approachability.

Visual direction

Modern, but warm

I used a softer, more elegant visual system with balanced spacing, subtle depth, and a refined color palette to keep the site feeling premium.

Warm neutrals Deep accent tones Glass-like panels Luxury styling
Site experience

Clearer, more inviting content

The structure highlights services, featured projects, testimonials, and contact information in a way that feels organized and easy to scan.

Better flow

Improved section order and pacing.

More trust

Clean styling supports credibility.

More premium

Luxury-inspired visual details.

More usable

Easier for visitors to take action.

Process

My redesign approach

I built the redesign around brand perception first, then refined the layout so the content feels more premium and intentional.

1
Step one

Identify the brand problem

I started by recognizing that the website needed to look more professional so it could better represent the quality of the company’s services.

2
Step two

Define a modern luxury style

The new direction uses elegant spacing, rich accent colors, and sophisticated presentation to create a more high-end feel.

3
Step three

Rebuild the structure

Sections were organized to guide visitors through the story more naturally, making it easier to understand the company and request a service.

Results

What the redesign delivers

The result is a site that feels more aligned with a premium landscape brand: cleaner, more trustworthy, and much more visually elevated.

Before direction

More functional than refined

The original presentation was serviceable, but it did not fully communicate the premium nature of the brand.

After direction

More polished and luxury-focused

The redesign introduces a refined visual style that feels modern, professional, and better suited to high-end outdoor spaces.

Key takeaway

A luxury brand should look like one.

This redesign makes the website feel more professional while keeping it welcoming and easy to navigate. The end result is a stronger online presence that matches the quality of the landscaping work.

Designed to feel modern, refined, and premium.
Final summary

Why this redesign works

The new direction gives Ricci's Landscape Management a cleaner visual identity, a more luxurious tone, and a layout that helps users trust the brand faster. It is professional without feeling cold, and modern without losing warmth.
