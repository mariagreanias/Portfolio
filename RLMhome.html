About

Rooted in Northwest Indiana Since 1994

Founded in 1994, Ricci's Landscape Management has grown from a neighborhood lawn service into one of Northwest Indiana's most trusted full-service landscape companies. What began with founder Dean Ricci mowing lawns in Lakes of the Four Seasons at just 10 years old has evolved into a team of more than 65 professionals delivering landscape design, construction, maintenance, irrigation, lighting, snow removal, and outdoor solutions for residential and commercial properties across the region.

For over three decades, RLM has remained committed to craftsmanship, professional training, and strong community ties. Through continued education, industry leadership, and meaningful local involvement, the team takes pride in delivering thoughtful outdoor spaces and dependable service that clients can rely on year after year.

RLM's success is rooted in professional pride, local knowledge, and a team culture focused on doing the work the right way. From large-scale commercial maintenance to luxury residential installations, every project is approached with the same care and attention to detail.
