Our Story

Started by a founder obsessed with the details.

Ember & Oak began with one goal: make coffee feel personal again. Our founder spent years learning how origin, roast development, and brewing technique can turn an ordinary cup into a meaningful ritual. That curiosity grew into a roasting studio and café that reflects the community around it—warm, welcoming, and crafted with care.
